About Me

"I'm a self taught front-end developer who specializes in creating responsive websites that allow the client to operate your webpage in the most efficient and hassle free manner. By employing progressive enhancement strategies, your website is distributed with a responsive blueprint that can best make use of the screen real estate available on the smallest to largest devices. Multiple browser compatibility is confirmed by utilizing feature recognition so older browsers still being used today deliver a trouble-free experience. Modern browsers can take it to the next level and heighten a users time spent on your site."
